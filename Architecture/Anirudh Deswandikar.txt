Aniruddha Deswandikar heads the Microsoft Technology Center (MTC) at Bangalore. The MTC engages with Microsoft Customers and Partners with a sole purpose of making them successful with their vision of building efficient enterprise systems on the Microsoft Platform. Through various offerings and practices, the MTC Architects help customers with Architecture and Technology Strategy for building their next generation systems and products across various Industry workloads. Aniruddha has 23 years of Industry Experience across startups  and large corporations. He has been with Microsoft for 9 years. Before Microsoft, he worked at Netflix.com as a senior developer where he helped build backend systems to support Netflix's Internet Scale business.
