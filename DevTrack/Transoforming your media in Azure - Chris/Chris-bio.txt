Chris has nearly 20 years of experience in the marketing and management of software and services, Chris is an expert with a successful track record in creating innovative solutions to the problems faced by both large companies and individuals. Starting in the 1990s, he worked on must-have multimedia products like DeBabelizer, Macromedia’s Director and Flash, and Internet video-compression tool Media Cleaner Pro (later Autodesk Cleaner). At Apple for 10 years, initially heading up its Final Cut efforts for EMEIA and helping it become the leading NLE, his role expanded over time to manage all of Apple’s desktop and mobile apps. He was instrumental in launching many Apple products, including, Final Cut Pro, Aperture, OS X, iOS versions of iLife and iWork, iPad, and iPhone.
